Solar System Simulation by Dan Hodgin and Savoy Schuler (2016)


Display Notes
-------------
Planetary orbits use the square root of the distance from the Sun.
The Sun is displayed at 1/10 its actual size, and Jupiter and Saturn are displayed at half their actual size.


Key Assignments
---------------

	r          	- Start/Suspend Animation
	Up Arrow   	-  Rotate Up
	Down Arrow 	- Rotate Down
	Left Arrow 	- Rotate Left
	Right Arrow	- Rotate Right
	a/d        	- Pan in X direction
	w/s        	- Pan in Y direction
	q/r        	- Zoom (Pan in Z direction)
	1/2        	-	 Speed of Animation
	3          	- Smooth/Flat Shading
	4          	- Wireframe/Polygon Rendering
	5          	- Texture Mapping
	6          	- Lighting
	f          	- Single Step Animation
	+/-        	- Inc/Dec Resolution
	Esc        	- Quit
